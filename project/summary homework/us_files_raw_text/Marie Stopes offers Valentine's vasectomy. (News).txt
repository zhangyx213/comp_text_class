Reproductive healthcare charity Marie Stopes International is offering people the chance to have the 'snip' at a discount rate this Valentine's Day, as well as enter a free prize draw for holiday vouchers. Black Cat has created the campaign, which is intended to raise awareness of the charity's vasectomy service. Press ads will direct traffic to the Marie Stopes website to obtain the 'vasectomy voucher'.
