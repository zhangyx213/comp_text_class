Boston, MA -- (ReleaseWire[1]) -- 11/10/2014 -- IRAMED GmbH (IRAMED) is a medical device company. The company conducts research and optimizes solutions in all aspects of interventional techniques covering many fields such as endoscopy, urology, radiology and cardiology. Its products include movystar, easyflex, movytax, flexystar, angiomarker, drainage, nasal bile duct drainage sets, transpapillary bile duct and drainage sets, among others. IRAMED provides solutions and designs products for particular customer problems with special attention on minimal invasive devices. The company offers vasectomy and nephrostomy for Neurology. It also offers intrauterine drainage-set for prenatal therapy. The company develops specialty products in cooperation with physicians from different faculties. IRAMED is headquartered in Wegberg-Arsbeck, Germany.

This report is a source for data, analysis, and actionable intelligence on the company's portfolio of pipeline products. The report provides key information about the company, its major products and brands.
