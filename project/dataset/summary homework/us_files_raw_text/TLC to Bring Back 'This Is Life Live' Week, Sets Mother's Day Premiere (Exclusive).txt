Apr 06, 2018( The Wrap: / Delivered by Newstex)  TLC's'This Is Life Live' is set to return next month, the network announced on Friday. The Discovery-owned network will air a second go of it's live event, which captures major milestones in the lives of four families, across four nights beginningSunday, May 13. Produced by Fly on the Wall Entertainment, each two-hour episode will follow personal journeys of families who are about to experience an extraordinary circumstance on live television. 
According to the network, 'whether these moments prove to be inspirational, shocking or heartbreaking, we'll follow the twists, turns and surprises that come along with putting unfiltered moments on television.'
