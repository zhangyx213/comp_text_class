A. Court hearing
B. Golf tournament
C. Softball game
D. Conference on abductions

A. Elbow B. Shoulder
C. Wrist D. Back

A. North St. Louis County
B. West St. Louis County
C. South St. Louis County
D. St. Charles County

A. Iceland B. Denmark
C. Norway D. Finland

A. Cubs B. Phillies C. Tigers D. Indians

A. Olive Garden B. Ruby Tuesday
C. Houlihan's D. Applebee's
7. Which of these toys was recalled last week for the second time in less than a year?
A. Tickle Me Elmo B. Easy Bake oven
C. Barbie D. GI Joe
8. Jim Nicholson resigned last week as secretary of what Cabinet department?
A. Interior B. Education
C. Energy D. Veterans Affairs

A. Madison Square Garden
B. Grand Central Station
C. Lincoln Center
D. Empire State Building
10. Newly released memos indicate that organized crime may have targeted what public figure in the 1970s?
A. Warren Burger B. Richard Nixon
C. Spiro Agnew D. Nelson Rockefeller
11. What candidate withdrew from the presidential race last week?
A. Ron Paul B. Chris Dodd
C. Jim Gilmore D. Tom Tancredo
12. Rhiannon O'Donnabhain is suing the IRS because it wouldn't allow a deduction for what kind of operation?
A. Sex change B. Vasectomy
C. Face lift D. Breast reduction
Answers
1. B. Shawn appeared at a golf tournament to raise money for his family's foundation.
2. A. Carpenter was scheduled for surgery on his right elbow.
3. C. They want to build a mosque on Lemay Ferry Road in South County.
4. A. Anheuser-Busch will distribute Icelandic Glacial water.
5. B. The Phillies loss to the Cardinals last Sunday was their 10,000th.
6. D. IHOP bought Applebee's.
7. B. Easy Bake ovens were recalled because of safety concerns.
8. D. Nicholson has been head of the Department of Veterans Affairs.
9. B. The pipe blast occurred near Grand Central Station.
10. A. The memos discuss whether crime figures were considering a hit on Chief Justice Burger.
11. C. Gilmore, the former governor of Virginia, dropped out of the race.
12. A. The deduction for a sex-change operation was denied.
