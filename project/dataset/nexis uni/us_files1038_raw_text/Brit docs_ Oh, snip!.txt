IT WAS the unkindest cut of all.
A British man hospitalized for a minor operation got a vasectomy by mistake.
Bungling doctors at Royal Liverpool Hospital accidentally snipped the tubes that carried sperm from his testicles to his penis - instead of performing a "minor urological procedure."
The hospital has "apologized unreservedly" for the February blunder, which it described as a "never event," the Liverpool Echo reported. The surgeon has been suspended. The patient has since undergone a reverse operation.
