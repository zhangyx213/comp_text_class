Friday-next Sunday: The Directors LLP launches its venture at the Music Box Theatre in Minneapolis. Led by producer/director Steve Barberio, the company has two major emphases: producing tours and managing the Music Box. In this case, Allison Moore's comedy about an older man whose young wife wants children will stop at the Music Box during a 14-city tour through Minnesota, Iowa and Wisconsin. Mary Fox plays Hope, the young wife who wants her hubby, Alan (Dan Hopman), to reverse his vasectomy and become once again a fountain of fertility. Others in the case include Shelby Flannery, Taj Ruler and Nathan Surprenant. Barberio directs. (Previews 7:30 p.m. Thu. Opens 7:30 p.m. Fri.-Sat., 2 p.m. next Sun. $10-$25. Ends Oct. 2. Music Box, 1407 Nicollet Av. S., Mpls. 612-424-1407. .)
