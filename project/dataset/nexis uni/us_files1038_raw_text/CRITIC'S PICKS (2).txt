Tops of the night
Jack (Kiefer Sutherland) ponders the last vial of virus; Chase (James Badge Dale) ponders his future; and Tony (Carlos Bernard) ponders the possibility of the death penalty for putting Michelle's life before national security in the third-season season finale of "24" at 8 p.m. on Channel 2.
Fox announced last week that the drama will return in January for a fourth season.
Also of note
6 p.m. on Cinemax: The documentary "And Along Came a Spider" tells a story of sex, drugs and murder in Iran.
7 p.m. on Channel 2: Fantasia Barrino and Diana DeGarmo sing for the crown on "American Idol." The winner will be named Wednesday.
7 p.m. on Channel 4: Gibbs (Mark Harmon) hunts terrorist Ari in the first-season finale of "Navy NCIS," which returns in the fall titled simply "NCIS."
8:30 p.m. on Channel 30: Jim (Jim Belushi) agrees to get a vasectomy in the third-season finale of "According to Jim."
