DON'T QUESTION Mayor de Blasio's expertise in wildlife birth control.
De Blasio Friday lashed out at a Staten Islander who suggested his support for deer vasectomies to combat the borough's wildlife population might stem more from NYCLASS' influence than science.
"Please leave your conspiracy theories at home. They have nothing to do with it," the mayor said to the man, who called into WNYC's Brian Lehrer show. "We carefully worked . . . with a host of wildlife experts."
